Champollion & The Birth of Egyptology

This site is named Champollion & The Birth of Egyptology because it highlights the extraordinary contributions of Jean-François Champollion, the man who unlocked the mystery of Egyptian hieroglyphs. The name reflects both the individual and the discipline he helped create, making it relevant, clear, and memorable for the project.

Site purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide an accessible and engaging introduction to Jean-François Champollion’s life and work. It will explain his passion for languages, his groundbreaking decipherment of Egyptian hieroglyphs, and the legacy he left as the founder of modern Egyptology. The site aims to inform and inspire visitors who are curious about history, language, and the mysteries of ancient Egypt.

Scenarios

  • How did Champollion decipher Egyptian hieroglyphs?
  • What is the significance of his work in modern Egyptology?
